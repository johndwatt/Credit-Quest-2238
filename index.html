WELCOME TO CREDIT QUEST: 2238

It is the year 2238. Humanity is testing new spaceships in its effort to become an intergalactic species. Fed up with the mundane day-to-day of Earth life, you spend your last credits on spaceship flight training and purchase your very own spaceship. You sign a contract to test the limits of spaceflight in certain uncharted areas of the galaxy in exchange for a steady flow of credits. Though the move is risky, and flying in uncharted areas can be dangerous, you know you can retire and live in comfort if you earn 1 million credits. With contract in hand, you ready yourself to take flight to the stars. There is just one last task before you speed off to earn your fortune...
